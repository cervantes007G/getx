Principles

PERFORMANCE

GetX is focused on performance and minimum consumption of resources. GetX does not use Streams or ChangeNotifier.

PRODUCTIVITY

GetX uses an easy and pleasant syntax. No matter what you want to do, there is always an easier way with GetX.

ORGANIZATION

GetX allows the total decoupling of the View, presentation logic, business logic, dependency injection, and navigation.
